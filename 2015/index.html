About

Tutorials in Theoretical Chemistry

The Tutorials in Theoretical Chemistry will be organized by the Université Paul Sabatier in Luchon - France, and held from the 1st to 6th February 2015.


These tutorials are focused in computer implementations, and numerical methods.


This winter school is done in the frame of the École des sciences avancées de Luchon.

Venue

Luchon Superbagneres (Ski resort at 1800m height)

The tutorials will take place in Luchon Superbagnères in France. Bagnères-de-Luchon, also referred to as Luchon is an authentic mountain town in the Pyrenees at the southernmost point of the Haute-Garonne Department, stands at the heart of a majestic mountain setting. It is surrounded by the mountain ranges 13 most famous peaks, including Aneto, which is the highest peak in the Pyrenees (3,404m).

Topics

  • Advanced programming techniques (T. Leininger)

  • Low-dimensional Carbon structures: the usefulness of simple approaches (S. Evangelisti)
  • Quantum Magnetism: the Heisenberg model (N. Suaud)
  • Quantum Dynamics (A. Berger; F. Gatti)

Luchon, natural sites

Luchon have many beautiful places to explore by foot, bicycle, motorcycle, horseback and by car. 1) The Lys Valley is a wild valley worth exploring up to the impressive Cascade d’Enfer waterfall and beyond, to the Gouffre d’Enfer sinkhole. 2) The Lac d’Oô is a blue-green gem of a lake at 1,507 m, surrounded by stunning vertical rock faces and fed by a 275m waterfall. 3) The Hospice de France is in a spectacular setting of mountain pastures and forests. It is also an historic place that has been welcoming travellers and adventurers exploring the Pyrenees since the 17th century.
