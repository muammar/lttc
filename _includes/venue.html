Venue

Luchon-Superbagnères (Ski resort at 1800m height)

The tutorials will take place in Luchon-Superbagnères in France. Bagnères-de-Luchon, also referred to as Luchon is an authentic mountain town in the Pyrenees at the southernmost point of the Haute-Garonne Department, stands at the heart of a majestic mountain setting. It is surrounded by the mountain ranges 13 most famous peaks, including Aneto, which is the highest peak in the Pyrenees (3,404m).
