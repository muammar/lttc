Venue

Luchon Superbagneres (Ski resort at 1800m height)

The tutorials will take place in Luchon Superbagnères in France.. This is a ski resort above the town of Bagnères de Luchon in the French department of Haute-Garonne in the Midi-Pyrénées region. It has a ski area located on a naturally balcony where you can see the valley of Luchon and the Maladeta massif, including Aneto, which is the highest peak in the Pyrenees.


The town is easily accessible by road (A64, exit Saint Gaudens), it is served by two airports, Toulouse Blagnac 140 km and Tarbes - Ossun - Lourdes 100 km. Also, a direct railway line linking the station to the Gare de Luchon Paris Austerlitz.
