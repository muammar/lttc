Venue

Centre de Vacances Le Néouvielle

The tutorials will take place in the Centre de Vacances Le Néouvielle that is located in Saint-Lary in Hautes-Pyrénées 80 km south of the département's capital Tarbes, next to the Le Néouvielle Nature Reserve and the Pyrenees National Park and is a 20 minute drive to Spain.

Important dates

We will meet at Blagnac Airport on 5th February at 16:00 (GMT+1). A bus will take us roundtrip from the airport to the venue as shown below:

  • Toulouse - Saint-Lary-Soulan : Departure from Blagnac airport on 5th February, 2016 at 17:00 (GMT+1).
  • Saint-Lary Soulan - Toulouse: Arrival on 13th February 2016 at 11:00 (GMT+1).
