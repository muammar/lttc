Tutorials in Theoretical Chemistry

The 2019 Edition of the Tutorials in Theoretical Chemistry organized by the Université Paul Sabatier will be held in Luchon-Superbagnères, France, from the 28th January to the 7th February 2019.


These tutorials are focused in computer implementations, and numerical methods. The number of students is limited to 26.




This event is done in the frame of École des sciences avancées de Luchon the mirror web page is at http://www.quantware.ups-tlse.fr/ecoledeluchon/sessionwis2/.
