Deadlines

Note: The maximum number of students for this edition is limited to 26. In order to be considered, please take into account the following dates:

  • Registration : before 31st October, 2017 at 23:59:59 (GMT+1).
  • Payment/Inscription: before 30th November 2017 at 23:59:59 (GMT+1).



1) Registration

Use the registration form to request your inscription to the tutorials. For general inquiries please use the contact form.

2) Payment/Inscription

If you are accepted, you will get an email with instructions to pay the inscription fees (100€).

3) Confirmation

You have to send us back the payment invoice, and once we receive it your participation is confirmed.




Note: Registration fees cover: teaching, meals, accommodation, and travel from Toulouse Blagnac to Luchon-Superbagnères.
