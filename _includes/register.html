How to proceed?

Inscription

Use the contact form to request your inscription to the tutorials.

Payment

If you are accepted, you will get an email with instructions to pay the inscription fees (50€).

Confirmation

You have to send us back the payment invoice, and once we receive it your participation is confirmed.
